About

About Somali Business Alliance

Building bridges between Somali entrepreneurs and global markets since 2023.

Our Mission

To empower Somali traders, producers, and small-to-medium businesses by creating trusted, transparent connections with domestic and international buyers — unlocking sustainable economic growth for Somalia.

Our Vision

A prosperous Somalia where every verified business can seamlessly access global markets, and international buyers have reliable, ethical access to high-quality Somali products.

Our Core Values

🌍 Integrity

Every member and listing is verified. We prioritize transparency and honesty in all trade connections.

🤝 Trust

We facilitate secure introductions and provide tools that help build long-term, reliable business relationships.

📈 Growth

Our platform is designed to help Somali businesses scale and expand beyond borders through strategic partnerships.

🌱 Community

We are proud to serve the Somali people and economy, and we believe in collective success.

Our Story

SBA was founded in 2023 by a group of young Somali entrepreneurs and tech enthusiasts who recognized the growing potential of Somalia’s emerging technology and electronics sector and the challenges businesses face in reaching international buyers.

Starting with a small group of solar energy and electronics traders in Mogadishu, we have grown into the leading B2B platform connecting over 2,450 tech-focused businesses across Somalia and the diaspora with buyers in more than 60 countries.

Today, SBA is recognized as a key facilitator of technology and electronics trade in East Africa, with offices in Mogadishu, Banaadir, and Nairobi.
