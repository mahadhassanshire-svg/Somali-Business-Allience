Frequently Asked Questions

Click a question to reveal the answer.

What is the Somali Business Alliance (SBA)?

SBA is a B2B platform that connects Somali tech companies, electronics traders, and businesses with local and international buyers. We verify members and facilitate trade opportunities across solar energy, smartphones, laptops, telecom equipment, and electronics.

Is it free to join SBA?

Yes! The Basic membership is completely free. You can create a profile, browse listings, and send up to 3 connection requests per month at no cost. Paid plans unlock unlimited access and premium features.

How do I connect with a buyer or supplier?

Go to the Connect page, fill out the inquiry form, and select your interests. Alternatively, browse listings on the Marketplace and click “Contact” on any product. Our team will facilitate introductions.

Are all listings verified?

Yes. Every supplier and buyer on the SBA platform is verified through document checks and, in many cases, physical verification. We maintain high standards to ensure trustworthy trade partners.

What products are commonly traded on SBA?

Our most active categories now include solar panels & inverters, smartphones and accessories, laptops & IT hardware, telecom equipment, networking gear, security systems, and consumer electronics.

How long does it take to get matched?

Most inquiries receive initial matches within 24–48 hours. Premium members often receive faster responses and priority matching.

Can international buyers join?

Absolutely. SBA is designed to connect international buyers with Somali producers. Many of our most active members are from the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kenya, Turkey, and Europe.

How do I update or remove my listing?

Log in to your account (after registration) and visit the Dashboard (coming soon). For now, simply email us at support@sballiance.so with your request.
